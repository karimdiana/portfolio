Who Am I

I'm Diana, pronounced like "Dee-anna". I'm a Computer Science major with a minor in Mathematics at Purdue University. My passion lies at the intersection of technology and design, where I strive to create intuitive digital experiences that solve real-world problems.

Throughout my academic journey, I've developed a strong foundation in algorithms, data structures, and software engineering principles. I'm particularly interested in front-end development, user experience design, and the application of AI in everyday technology.

This year has been a rollercoaster. I've learned so much about myself and my capabilities. I've tried myself in hackatons and worked as SWE but my heart lies in more creative pursuits.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me exploring photography, cooking or creating memes for my memepage.
